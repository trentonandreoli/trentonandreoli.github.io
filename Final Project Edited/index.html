Welcome to The Bouldering Community Webpage!


We believe climbing is more fun with friends, come climb with us every Friday from 5pm - 7pm!


We are a Indiana University registered organizaiton and work closely with Outdoor Adventures to provide all students on campus the oppurtunity to get introduced to the sport of bouldering.


We are meetup in the Outdoor Adventures Bouldering wall in the bottom floor of Eigenmann Hall. Directions on how to find us can base found on the "About" page.
