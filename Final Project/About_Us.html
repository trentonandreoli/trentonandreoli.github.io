Frequently Asked Questions!

What are we?

We are a free on campus climbing club. We focus on the community aspect and welcome climbers of all skill levels. We love to get new people into the sport and provide a constructive and safe environment for all. During our climbing hours the climbing gym on campus is free to everyone (member or not).

When do we meet?

We meet in the Outdoor Adventures bouldering wall located in the lowest level of Eigenmann Hall. Visual instructions are attached at the bottom of this page.

Where do we meet?

We host weekly gatherings in the Outdoor Adventures bouldering room from 5pm - 7pm every Friday during the school semester. We also run various bouldering and non-bouldering community events periodically. Check the Events tab for more information!

Do we cost anything?

No! We are completely free, our great friends at Outdoor Adventures have made bouldering at their wall free during club hours. It doesn't matter if you're a member or not, come climb with us!

How can I get involved?

There are several ways you reach us to join! You can check the contact tab to join our club groupme, to officially join us as a member on BeInvolved, or you can email one of our leaders to inquire more about us! If you're on the fence at all, try us out by showing up to a weekly gathering, it's free and we love welcoming new faces.
